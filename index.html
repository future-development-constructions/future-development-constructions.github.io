New build: house, townhouse and duplex

We always focuses specifically on new residential builds. New builds are generally fast and cost effective and are built to suit the Australian Standards.

We will collaborate closely with consultants and building professionals that aid in being cost, design and time effective. If we are engaged in the early stage of the project, we can help in the design process to ensure that our customers are designing a building that is minimal maintenance, strong and durable, while optimising the budget.

Townhouse and duplex are an excellent choice for an affordable entry to the property market. You benefit from the lower maintenance and upkeep costs of a smaller block of land, while still enjoying the privacy of your own home.

As always, we are proud of being an expert in townhouse and duplex design. With experience, we will help the clients build a home that is as individual as they are.

Granny flats

We can help you with utilising dead and unused space in your yard. Most councils allow a granny flat up to 60m2 without the need to deal with the council for your approval. However, you will need a plan via an architect, and you will need to engage a private certifier to receive your complying development certificate.

We can aid in the design and layout to ensure it is practical and energy efficient, we can assess your existing homes services such as mains meter box to assess which upgrades will be needed, also your services such as water, gas and stormwater connection points.

Outdoor structures such as carports, garages, studios, home gyms and the like all fall into this category and can be approached in the same manner.

FRIENDLY CONSULTATION

We will give many advice as possible to support clients with any decisions making for our services. Below is a list of some tips which you can use to consider before starting any projects:

  • What do you want to do to your home or lands, such as rebuild, build new house, townhouse, granny flat or else?
  • How would you like us to improve our home? What is your budget?
  • What do you consider getting the best outcome from us?
  • How do you maximize the space to build all the rooms?
